About us

ART OF SCIENCE

As there is air everywhere, there is science. It’s just a matter of becoming aware and looking at the things little differently. For developing scientific attitude it’s important to nurture mindset of enquiry. Through Art of Science project we provide opportunity of performing science practical experiments to curious minds. We orient students for day-to-day applications of science concepts. Scientific temperament has a power to make a significant difference in the life.
