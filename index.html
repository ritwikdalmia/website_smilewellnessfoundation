Smile Wellness Foundation (SWF) aims to translate science and transform minds by working with corporations, government agencies, colleges, underprivileged communities, and various other organizations that serve children by educating them. SWF wants to encourage people to use lifestyle changes and stress release tools for optimal performance.

MESSAGE FROM FOUNDER

Dr. Mrs. Surekha S. Bhalerao

 

When the call from within became obvious, I decided to choose a different path. Whatever little knowledge I had acquired, with the Best out of that I wanted to reach out to society. To be able to reach out on the larger scale, I registered SMILE Wellness Foundation (SWF). The main aim is to spread SMILEs and promote Wellness by educating people and communities, about physical, emotional and spiritual health. I look forward to translate science for transforming minds by providing complementary science education solutions. The ultimate vision of this program is to bring knowledge, unshakable SMILE, Peace and Harmony. As more and more hands are joining for this noble cause like my Core Team many organizations are taking active interest in taking the mission SMILE ahead. It feels very happy to witness that SWF is spreading its wings for changing millions of lives!
